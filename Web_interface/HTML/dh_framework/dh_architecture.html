Welcome to the website for the project: 16th Century Exegesis of Paul. The aim of this site is to provide a digital library of the reformed commentaries on Paul's epistles in the 16th century, with access to their PDF and XML-TEI versions. The results of topic-modeling will also be integrated, enabling a comparison of the epistles by verse (summer 2025).

Terms of Use and Citation The citation terms are as follows : "16th Century Exegesis of Paul - a Digital Library: 16th Century Exegesis of Paul, SNF207696, Universties of Geneva and Zürich, dir. Ueli Zahnd and Stefan Krauter, [date of consultation]".
